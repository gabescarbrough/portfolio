About Me

Problem Solving

The best way to solve a hard problem is to break it into many simple problems. I am a methodical developer who writes clear and semantic code, and I enjoy collaborating on teams where everyone adds their perspective and expertise to help their teammates grow.

Communication

Before I took the leap to pursue web development full-time I worked at a small non-profit where I did everything from produce a national award-winning PSA to write press releases which were picked up by major publications. I believe clear communication is key to creating great work.

Creativity

Whether it be designing packaging for a friend's album, producing electronic music or directing a short film, I always have an artistic pursuit going. As much as I enjoy writing AJAX calls to an API, I also love coding generative art.
